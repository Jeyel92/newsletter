Global Compliance Series, São Paulo edition, promoted by Sovos, had the purpose of 'harmonizing' the tax obligations

In last December, during dinner in the private room of the Cantaloup restaurant in São Paulo, Sovos provided guidance on the fiscal calendar for the coming years. The Global Compliance Series (GCS), São Paulo edition, talked about the tax obligations in vogue until 2022. The event counted with the presence of the tax lawyer Luciana Ferreira, Sovos' leaders in Brazil and a select group of guests.

Sovos has been expanding its operations in Latin America, with solutions in the cloud for several countries, perfectly suited to meet the next obligations, such as PAC Peru legislation and the NFS-e in Brazil: the new layout of the invoice, NF -e 4.0, EFD-Reinf and Block K, which involves the stockholding areas of ​​companies.

VIANEWS Hotwire covered the event for developing content for the Sovos website in Brazil and posts for social media channels.
